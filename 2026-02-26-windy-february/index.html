Windy February

📆 Feb 26, 2026
🏷 spain

Weather has limited my cycling, mainly due to the wind being so strong not ideal as I am trying to get some good miles in for the Millars Gran Fondo next month with Garth. Needed Flo to pick me up in Turis as the wind became dangerous

We have a few spare Euros from the house sale allowing us to started the process of upgrading our solar by adding a 3.5kw battery, next will be the inverter, we also ordered a fairly large Shed, and made a start to clearing the ground for Geocell and stones, also I moved the wood store and gabons.

After visiting the Hospital Flo got a date for Pre op end of March.

Off on our trip to Valencia we needed to rethink parking as Torrent was full due to the start of Fallas, ended up driving to EL Saler parking in the shopping centre and walking to the Hotel great day for a walk. Great couple of days in Valencia lots of shopping, we need practice at holidaying.

Friday we meet with a Solicitor to help with the process of getting our Residencia made permanent by the Police Local in Alzara all went well.
