Atlantic Drive Route 1

📆 Sep 27, 2013
🏷 travel cycling

Near perfect conditions for our bike ride today little wind and very warm. The ride turned out to be a lot easier than I thought.

Hard to believe shorts no arm warmers at the end of Sept and it was 21c at one time. We decided not to go to Keel as it is all on the main road. Luckily the route brought us to the coffee shop where we watched the world go by.

When Frank and Kath arrived we drove what we cycled this morning and then went up to the mast where this year the weather was great and the views fabulous
