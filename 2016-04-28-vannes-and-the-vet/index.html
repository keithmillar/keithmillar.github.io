Vannes and the Vet

🏷 spain

Both Flo and I spent the night running to the toilet luckily we where parked right beside the toilets and they where heated.

{{ more }} Feeling a bit off we all set off to the vet, a bit different from home only one vet no receptionist, anyway all went well and Lotti can now travel.
