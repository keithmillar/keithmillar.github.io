Valencia Marathon

📆 Dec 01, 2024
🏷 running

Garth and Helena treated Flo and I to three nights in the Medium Hotel Valencia. We he had a couple of days to explore round Valencia and go to the Marathon Expo which was much better than last year. On Friday once finish with Garth and Helena, Flo and I took the tram back in to the city were we found a fab little bar which also sold Vinyl LP’s and CD’s with music posters adorning the walls a good find. Saturday was the Bimbo run which we Half heartily took part in and once it was finished we headed for breakfast in the hotel then home.
